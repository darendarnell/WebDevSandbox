Mashed Potatoes

Description

These garlic mashed potatoes are DELICIOUS and the perfect side dish for your dinner tonight.

A beautiful marriage between potatoes, butter, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds potatoes
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup whole milk

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes
  2. Combine butter and milk on stovetop
  3. Mince garlic
  4. Mash soft and boiled potatoes
  5. Combine potatoes, garlic, milk, butter, and salt
